SandWich

Making of Sandwich Filling:-

  1. Boil Potato(Aloo),peel the boiled potatoes , then mesh them in a bowl or in any utensil
  2. Heat oil in kadhai or in frying pan ,after your oil is heated put cumin's in that oil and stir it for 30seconds
  3. Then add Mashed potatoes in oil , then add Dhana Powder,Salt,Turmeric Powder,Red chilli Powder
  4. Then mix them well in high flame untill potato turn a little dark in color
  5. Now your Filling is ready,keep it aside.

   Steps:-

  1. Take a bread Put filling on that and put another bread over the filling and press a little not too hard
  2. Take a frying pan and spread a little oil in pan.
  3. Then Put breads filled with mixture on the pan in low flame untill they turn a dark (be carefull keep checking it can also burn)
  4. Then roll over (Sandwich Paltaen)and let other side also to turn dark
  5. Now your sandwich is ready , you can make more sandwich like this
  6. Serve it with Tomato Ketchup or chutney.

Ingredients:-

  • 250g Potato(Aloo)
  • 2 TeaSpoon Oil
  • 1 Pinch of Cumin Seeds(Jeera)
  • Turmeric Powder(According to your taste)
  • Salt (According to your taste)
  • Dhana Powder (According to your taste)
  • Bread (According to quantity)
  • Red chilli Powder (According to your taste)
