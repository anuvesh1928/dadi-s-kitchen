Aloo Stuffed Idli

   Steps:-

  1. Heat oil in a pan , then add Cumin seeds and Curry leaves in it and temper them.
  2. Then roast ginger paste,green chilli and all the spices in that.
  3. Now mix mashed and boiled potatoes in that.
  4. Now cool it down and then make small muffins,spread oil in Idli mold and fill 1/3 part.
  5. Then place one-one muffing in center and cover it with batter.
  6. Now in Idli's Utensil or in Cooker heat water and cook them in steam for 8-10 minutes.
  7. Your Aloo stuffes Idli's are ready , serve them with coconut chutney.

Ingredients:-

  • 2 Katori Idli Batter
  • 1/2 Katori Boiled and Mashed Potatoes(Aloo)
  • 1/2 Small Spoon Red chilli Powder
  • 1 Pinch of Asafoetida(Hing)
  • 1/2 Small Spoon Cumin Seeds(Jeera)
  • 3-4 Curry Leaves(Kadi Patta)
  • 1 Pinch of Amchur Powder
  • 1/2 Small Spoon Ginger Paste
  • 1 Small Spoon Oil
  • 1/2 Small Spoon Choped Green Chillies
  • Salt (According To your Taste)
