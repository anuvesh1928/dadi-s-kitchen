Spring-Roll

Making of Filling

Take a bowl and add potatoes ,chopped or mashed paneer,carrot,capsicum,onion,Salt,peas,Red chilli powder and chaat masala , now mix them well and your filling is ready.

   Steps:-

  1. Mix wheat flour and rava in a bowl
  2. Add 2 big spoon of oil,Salt,Carom seeds,1 pinch of red chilli powder and a little bit water , now churn(gund len)it and make a tightly Dough
  3. Now make dough balls and chapati them like chapati.
  4. Now sprinkle a little flour on chapati and put filling on that .
  5. Put a little water on the corner of the chapati and now roll it
  6. Now also stick the open sides also with water
  7. You can make other rolls like this,then fry them in hot oil.
  8. Now cut them in 2-3 pices after it get's fried.
  9. Serve them with

Ingredients:-

  • 1 Cup(Katori) Wheat Flour(Gehun ka aata)
  • 2 TeaSpoon Rava(Sooji)
  • Oil (For Frying)
  • 2 Potato (Boiled and Mashed)
  • A little bit Paneer
  • 1 chopped Onion(Pyazz)
  • 1 chopped Capsicum(Shimla Mirch)
  • A little bit Peas(Hare Matar)
  • 1 Chopped Carrot(Gajar)
  • Salt (According to your taste)
  • 1/2 small spoon Chaat Masala
  • 1/2 small spoon Red chilli Powder
  • 1 pinch of carom seeds(Ajwain)
