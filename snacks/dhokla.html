SOOJI  DHOKLA

   Steps:-

  1. Put Rava In A Bowl , then add curd and water and mix them.Keep it for 30minutes.
  2. After 30min Add Salt,Turmeric,Soda and Baking Powder in that Paste.
  3. Boil water in Dhokla Stand , Spread cooking oil in Dhokla Tray , add paste in Dhokla Tray , Put that Tray in High Flame for around 7min
  4. Take the Tray out of stove , cut them in to Pieces.
  5. Heat a Kadhai , then put oil in that kadhai.
  6. After oil get heated , then put Coriandar Leaves,Cumin Seeds,Mustard seeds,Curry Leaves in Kadhai , then add Dhokla pieces in kadhai and mix them for around 1min.
  7. Take them out of kadhai.
  8. Serve Hot and Delicious Dhokle with chutney or ketchup.

Ingredients:-

  • 500g Rava(Sooji)
  • 250g Curd(Dahi)
  • 1/4 TeaSpoon Turmeric(Haldi)
  • 4 TeaSpoon Oil
  • 1/4 TeaSpoon Soda
  • 1/2 TeaSpoon Baking Powder
  • 2 TeaSpoon Mustard Seeds(Rai)
  • 1 TeaSpoon Cumin Seeds(Jeera)
  • Green Chilli (According to Your Taste)
  • Coriander Leaves(According to Your Taste)
  • 12-14 Curry Leaves (Meethee Neem)
  • Salt (According To Your Taste)
  • 1 and 1/2 Cup Normal Water
