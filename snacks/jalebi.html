Jalebi

Making Of Sugar Syrup

  1. Take one pan and fill it with water and then add sugar and mix then well in high flame
  2. Heat it till it is highly boiled.

   Steps:-

  1. Put flour and curd in one Bowl , then mix it well.After mixing keep it aside for 1Hour
  2. And add soda in mixture and then also add food color powder.
  3. Heat ghee in kadhai .
  4. Make a cone of pollythin , fill mixture inside the cone and cut a little bit from bottom to release the mixture.
  5. Press the cone and make a shape of jalebi with mixture.
  6. Then fry it till it changes it color to little golden.
  7. Now Take it out of kadhai and Dip then in Sugar Syrup for 1min.
  8. Now Serve It . Your delicious,Gorgeous,yummy Jalebi's are ready.

Ingredients:-

  • 2 Cup Flour(Maida)
  • 1 Cup Curd(Dahi)
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • Half TeaSpoon Baking Soda
  • Ghee (for frying)
  • Half TeaSpoon Jalebi food color powder
  • 2 cup sugar
  • 1 and half cup water
